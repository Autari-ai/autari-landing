# Autari section-background storyboard — one continuous humanized story

Each major section gets its OWN cinematic background image/gif, but together they tell ONE story: a
business owner's arc from **drowning in busywork → getting their time back**. All shot photoreal, real
people, 4K, documentary-cinematic, unified dark **teal-and-green** color grade, 35mm, shallow depth of
field, film grain. Time-of-day arc carries the narrative. Animate with **Google Veo 3** (better human
realism than Kling). GIFs are seamless **ping-pong loops** (last frame = first).

Continuity rules: same tonal grade + film look everywhere; same modern-office world; recurring "founder"
character where possible; generous negative space for the section's text; NO on-screen text/logos.

## Shot list / prompts (nano_banana_pro stills → Veo 3 motion → ping-pong GIF)
- **1 Problem:** late night, a tired business owner at a cluttered desk buried under stacks of paperwork and glowing screens, head resting on hand, warm desk lamp + subtle green screen glow, deep shadows, city night through the window. Centered negative space.
- **2 What We Automate:** wide establishing shot of a calm empty modern open-plan office at night, rows of desks with softly glowing emerald-green screens, empty chairs, city lights through the glass, volumetric haze. Lots of negative space (cards sit on top).
- **3 How It Works:** two colleagues at a table in a modern dark office reviewing a process on a laptop together, one gesturing, warm + green light, bokeh. Side negative space.
- **4 Survey:** close-up of hands opening and starting to type on a sleek laptop in a dark modern space, warm green screen glow illuminating the hands and face softly, bokeh. Negative space.
- **5 FAQ:** two professionals in a relaxed, warm conversation over coffee in a dark modern office, subtle green accent, bokeh, trust and calm. Negative space.
- **6 Final CTA:** golden-dusk, a relaxed business owner looking out at the city with a calm smile / stepping away from the desk, warm golden + subtle green rim light, bokeh, hopeful resolution. Centered negative space.

## Motion (Veo 3, subtle, seamless)
Each ~5s, locked or very slow push, subtle human motion (breath, glance, gesture, typing, steam rising),
ambient bokeh twinkle. Preserve faces/identity, no morphing, no cuts, no text. Convert to ping-pong GIF so
it loops invisibly.

## Wiring
Each section renders its GIF as a full-bleed background at low opacity behind an ink gradient scrim, so the
section text/cards stay legible and the story reads as you scroll. Reduced-motion → the still `.jpg`.
